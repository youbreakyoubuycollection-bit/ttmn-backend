FULL COMMUNICATION WORKFLOW (STEP-BY-STEP)

Step 1: Inquiry Received   
You receive a message through your website, email, social media, or order form. 
 
Step 2: Send Inquiry Response   
Use the ready-made template to respond warmly and gather details. 

Step 3: Prepare and Send Quote   
Provide a clear, itemized quote with deposit requirements and timeline. 
 
Step 4: Deposit Paid   
No deposit - no work begins. This locks in the project. 
 
Step 5: Create and Send Mockup   
You prepare the design and send the first draft to the client. 
 
Step 6: Receive Revision Notes   
Clients send grouped notes. One revision round at a time. 

Step 7: Client Approves Final Design   
Approval must be written. No changes afterward. 
 
Step 8: Production Begins   
You create the item(s) exactly as approved. 
 
Step 9: Order Completion   
Send notice that the order is done and attach a balance reminder. 

Step 10: Pickup or Delivery   
Schedule fulfillment after payment is confirmed. 
 
Step 11: Review Request   
Ask for feedback once the order is delivered.
